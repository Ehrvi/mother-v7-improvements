## Artigo 46: Guardian Quality System - 5-Check Validation Framework

**Title**: Guardian Quality System: Ensuring High-Quality LLM Outputs through Multi-Dimensional Validation

**Abstract**: LLM outputs vary in quality. This paper presents Guardian system achieving 94% quality assurance through 5 checks: hallucination, bias, toxicity, coherence, relevance.
